Choosing step size for Inexact ALM algorithm

  • Show All Code
  • Hide All Code
Author

Saikat Banerjee

Published

July 28, 2023

Abstract
Try two different methods proposed for updating the penalty, which is equivalent to the step size. (1) Lin et. al. proposed a monotonically increasing step size. (2) Boyd proposed to dial the step size based on the primal and dual residuals

About

There are two alternate proposed methods for updating the penalty \mu on the residual. The penalty is equivalent to the step size. Here, we compare the two methods on a simulated dataset. The proposed methods are:

  • Eq. 25 of Lin et. al.
  • Eq. 3.13 of Boyd et. al.

To-Do: Check stopping criterion of Boyd et. al. using Eq. 3.12.
